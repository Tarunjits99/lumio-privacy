Lumio Together

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: March 27, 2026
For Couples · 18+ App
Lumio Together is a private couple connectivity app — designed exclusively for two paired partners. It is not a social network, not a public platform, and your data is never shared with advertisers or other users. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how it is protected.
🌙

Who We Are

Lumio Together is a private couple app for Android and iOS, designed for adults in romantic relationships. The app pairs exactly two users via a unique 6-character invite code and provides a fully private shared space — it is non-functional without a confirmed partner pairing.

⚠️ Lumio Together is intended for users aged 18 and older due to its intimate relationship content including mood sharing, menstrual cycle tracking, and romantic date planning.
📦

Information We Collect

From your sign-in provider (Google or Apple):

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo URL (if available)

During setup and pairing:

  • Gender (male / female / prefer not to say)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token (for push notifications)
  • 6-character invite code (auto-generated)
  • Timestamps (account creation, last seen)

Optionally provided by you:

  • Custom avatar photo (from your photo library)
  • Partner nickname, city, anniversary date
  • Menstrual cycle data — only if you choose to enable and share it

User-generated content (private to your couple):

  • Chat messages (text)
  • Scrapbook photos and voice notes
  • Mood entries, daily challenge answers
  • Grocery, errand, wishlist, and bucket list items
  • Game responses (trivia, Would You Rather, etc.)
✅ All content is strictly private to your couple. Nothing is publicly visible or shared with any other user.
🩺

Sensitive Health Data

Lumio Together includes an optional menstrual cycle and health tracking feature. This is sensitive health information and is handled with the highest care:

  • Entirely opt-in — disabled by default
  • A Share With Partner toggle controls whether your partner can see this data
  • If sharing is off, data is visible to you only
  • Stored in your private Firebase Firestore couple database — never shared with any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time from within the app or by deleting your account
📷

Camera, Microphone & Photo Library

Photo Library: Used to select your avatar photo and add images to your shared scrapbook. Images are stored privately within your couple's data.

Microphone: Used to record voice notes in the scrapbook. Audio is stored privately within your couple's space.

✅ The app does not make video calls, voice calls, or live camera streams. No media is ever uploaded publicly.

Permissions declared:

  • Android: CAMERA, RECORD_AUDIO, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
  • iOS: NSCameraUsageDescription, NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription, NSMicrophoneUsageDescription
📍

Location Data

✅ Lumio Together does not access your GPS or location at any time. No location permissions are requested or used.

The "Walk Together" feature uses your device's pedometer (step sensor) only — not GPS — to sync step counts between partners.

🔔

Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and Notifee to send push notifications. You may receive notifications for:

  • Partner answering the daily challenge
  • Partner sending a virtual gift or cuddle
  • Partner starting a Together activity (movie, walk, study session)
  • Dinner date reminders
  • Streak warnings (when your daily streak is about to expire)

You can manage or disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

💎

Virtual Currency (LBPoints)

Lumio Together uses a virtual in-app currency called LBPoints, earned by using the app (completing challenges, maintaining streaks, hourly usage). LBPoints are spent on virtual gifts sent to your partner.

✅ There are no real-money purchases, no subscriptions, no Apple IAP, and no Google Play Billing. No payment or financial data is ever collected.
🔧

🎯

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and couple pairing
  • To deliver core features (chat, activities, games, scrapbook, shared lists)
  • To send push notifications related to your couple's activity
  • To sync shared data between you and your partner in real time
  • To track LBPoints balance and virtual gift history
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations
🔗

Data Sharing

✅ We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers, marketers, or data brokers.

Data is shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your partner: Shared features (chat, lists, moods, scrapbook, games) are visible to your paired partner only.
  • With Firebase/Google: As our infrastructure provider. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
🗑️

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

  • All account data, couple pairing, and user-generated content will be permanently removed
  • Residual data in backups may persist for up to 90 days before full deletion
🔒

Data Security

  • Firebase Firestore access rules restrict all reads/writes strictly to the authenticated couple
  • Authentication is handled via Google and Apple — we never store passwords
  • Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs have access to your data
🔞

Children's Privacy

Lumio Together is intended exclusively for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware a minor has registered, we will promptly delete their account. If you believe a minor has accessed the app, please contact us immediately.

✅

Your Rights

Depending on your location (GDPR in the EU, PIPEDA in Canada, CCPA in California), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and all personal data
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as health data sharing
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us below.
